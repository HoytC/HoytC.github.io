A Brief Introduction

Greetings, and welcome to Smash Lab! The goal of this website is provide basic information on the video game series Super Smash Bros, and to honor it in all its glory.

On this site you will find a brief description of the origins of the game as well as information about its development into a competitive fighting game. Additionally, I will be humble-bragging in one of the nerdiest manners possible by describing my history and passion for Smashin' ... ehem.

You can further navigate the website by clicking the various links along the banner at the top of the site, or by clicking the cute pixel art character heads to access video tutorials, forums and footage of this wonderful game.

When you are finished browsing around please take a moment to visit the citations and appreciation page. None of the artwork on this site was created by me although it all falls under the public domain and/or has been presented within the terms of use by the original authors. I merely edited, cropped and adjusted the media, as well as recorded my own gameplay to provide the GIF animations found on the "Some Nerd" page.
